Fresh Excellence from Kenya's Heartland

Taonga Group is a trusted horticultural producer and sourcing partner based in Nanyuki, Kenya. With strategically located farms across Nanyuki, Mutara, Nakuru, and Narok, we cultivate premium-quality horticultural products using 100% safe sustainable agricultural practices

Why Partner With Us

Geographic Diversity: Farms across multiple agro-ecological zones enable year-round supply consistency.
Commitment to Safety: Every product meets strict safety standards, prioritizing consumer health and environmental stewardship.
Streamlined Supply Chain: Farm-to-buyer efficiency reduces costs and maximizes freshness.
